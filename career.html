Cristiano Ronaldo - Career Timeline

Manchester United (2003-2009)

Ronaldo joined Manchester United from Sporting CP and spent six glorious years at Old Trafford. During this period, he won 3 Premier League titles, 1 UEFA Champions League, and numerous individual awards.

Real Madrid (2009-2018)

In 2009, Ronaldo signed with Real Madrid. His nine years at the Bernabéu were marked by unprecedented success with 4 UEFA Champions League titles and over 450 goals.

Juventus (2018-2021)

Ronaldo joined the Italian giants Juventus, bringing his winning mentality to Serie A with 2 Serie A titles.

Manchester United Return (2021-2022)

Ronaldo returned to his former club Manchester United for one final season before moving to Saudi Arabia.

International Career

For Portugal, Ronaldo is the all-time leading goalscorer with over 130 goals. He led Portugal to victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship.
